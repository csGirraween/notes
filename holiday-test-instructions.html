Holiday Test Instructions

Welcome to the CS Education Girraween Holiday Test page. This page contains all the instructions you need to complete your holiday test successfully.

General Instructions

  • Complete all sections of the test within the allocated time.
  • Make sure to check your answers before submitting.
  • Use your notebook for any working out.

Mathematics Section

The mathematics section consists of 26 questions covering various topics including:

  • Number theory and operations
  • Fractions, decimals, and percentages
  • Algebra and patterns
  • Geometry and measurement
  • Problem-solving and logical reasoning

Some questions reference diagrams. Pay careful attention to the information provided in these diagrams.

Important: This is made by CS Girraween and all questions are for you to keep!

Writing Section

The writing section will test your ability to express ideas clearly and effectively in written form.

Format:

  • You will be given a prompt or topic to write about.
  • Your response should be well-structured with a clear introduction, body, and conclusion.
  • Pay attention to grammar, spelling, and punctuation.
  • Use appropriate language and vocabulary for the given task.

Reminder: Review the writing tips page for detailed guidance on different writing styles and language techniques.

Thinking Skills Section

The thinking skills section consists of 30 questions designed to assess your critical thinking and logical reasoning abilities. This section covers:

  • Pattern recognition and sequence completion
  • Logical deduction and inference
  • Spatial reasoning and visualization
  • Critical analysis of statements and arguments
  • Problem-solving with abstract concepts

Questions may include diagrams, patterns, or logical scenarios that require careful analysis before selecting the correct answer.

Tip: Manage your time carefully - aim to spend about 1 minute per question to complete all 30 questions.

Submission Instructions

Please follow these guidelines when submitting your completed test:

  1. Ensure your name is clearly typed on all tests.
  2. Attempt all questions within the time limit.
  3. Try your BEST!

If you have any questions about accessing the holiday test, please ask your teacher during class or contact CS Girraween.

Good luck with your holiday test! Remember to use all the resources and strategies you've learned in class.

For test solutions, visit the Holiday Test Solutions page after completing your test. You'll need the access code provided by your teacher.
